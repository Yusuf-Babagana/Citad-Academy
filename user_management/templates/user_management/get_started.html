Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Choose your role and follow the registration links above.

Is there a fee?

Free for now.

How can a school admin register their school?

To register your school, log in as a school admin, and go to the "Registration" section. Follow the prompts to provide necessary details about your school.

What steps does a school admin take after logging in?

After logging in, a school admin can create classes, subjects/courses, register students, register staff (teachers), and manage various aspects of school administration.

How does a school admin assign classes, subjects, and students to teachers?

After creating classes and subjects, a school admin can assign classes to teachers and students. This is done through the "Assignments" section in the admin dashboard.

How can schools register their students and staff?

Schools can register students and staff (teachers) through the "Registration" section in the admin dashboard. Provide the required information for each student or staff member.

What if there's an enrollment mistake? How can schools correct it?

If there's an enrollment mistake, school admins can review and approve enrollment requests in the "Enrollment Approval" section to correct any errors.

How do independent learners create their accounts?

Independent learners can create their accounts by clicking on the "Register as Independent Learner" button on the homepage. Follow the registration process and provide the necessary information.
